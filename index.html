Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

ClariaDay ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ClariaDay mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (used solely for account authentication)

Passwords are securely stored using industry-standard encryption and are never visible to us in plain text. ClariaDay does not collect sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide app functionality and customer support
  • Enable premium subscriptions
  • Communicate important service-related updates
  • Maintain app security and performance

3. Third-Party Services

ClariaDay uses Apple’s App Store and In-App Purchase system to process subscriptions and payments. We do not use third-party analytics services that collect personal data.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service or to comply with legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including secure storage, encryption, and restricted access.

6. Data Sharing

ClariaDay does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

7. Your Rights (GDPR – EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact:
